❤️ Siddhi ❤️

Jab tum saath hoti ho,
toh duniya thodi easy lagti hai.

Aaj Propose Day pe koi bade-bade promises nahi,
bas dil se ye kehna hai —
main apni poori life tumhare saath hi chalna chahta hoon.

Main ye nahi kehta ki sab kuch hamesha perfect hoga,
par har mushkil mein tera haath thaam ke khada rahunga.

Tumhari muskaan meri taqat hai,
aur tumhari khamoshi bhi mujhe apna sa lagti hai.
