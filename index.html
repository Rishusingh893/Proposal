Happy Valentine’s Day Siddhi ❤️

I love you… aur mujhe roz ye bolne ki zarurat nahi padti, kyunki tum already jaanti ho. Par phir bhi main har din tumhe ye feel karwana chahta hu ki meri life me tumhari jagah sabse alag hai. Main perfect nahi hu. Kabhi possessive ho jata hu, kabhi mood off hota hai… Lekin ek cheez ka promise hai — main kabhi tumhara trust break nahi karunga. Chahe fights ho, misunderstandings ho, tough days ho… main hamesha tumhara haath pakad ke chalna chahta hu. Tum sirf meri present nahi, meri future ka bhi part ho. Aaj pehla Valentine hai humara… aur shayad hum paas nahi hain, par dil se main sirf tumhare saath hu. Bas itna yaad rakhna — tum akeli nahi ho. Main hamesha tumhare saath hu. Tumhari smile meri sabse favourite cheez hai ❤️
